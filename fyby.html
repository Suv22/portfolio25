For Youth, By Youth

Founded by Medhavi and Shagun Katiyar (2020), For Youth, By Youth emerged in response to the disarray faced by students during the COVID-19 pandemic—a time when the usual rhythms of campus life fell silent, and the organic exchange of knowledge and experiences was abruptly interrupted. In this void, the initiative sought to restore a sense of direction, offering students a platform to make informed decisions and regain clarity in their academic and personal journeys. Through a series of mentorship sessions, peer-led guidance, and candid discussions on the realities of adulting—spanning career choices, mental health, and personal growth—the initiative became a lifeline for over 2,500 students in just one year. In the face of uncertainty, For Youth, By Youth provided the tools, connections, and insights necessary to navigate a world that seemed to have paused.
